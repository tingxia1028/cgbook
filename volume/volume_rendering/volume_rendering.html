properties of volumes

  • what are volumes

    Volumes, are collections of particles, ranging from atoms and molecules to any particle size.

  • collision probability the chance of a photon collision
    • probability density of collision per unit distance deﬁned by a coeﬃcient σ(x), which is the probability density of collision per unit distance traveled inside the volume. The physical unit of a collision coeﬃcient is inverse length.
    • mean free path the average distance traveled between collisions.

  • Volume Rendering Equation(VRE)

    describes the radiance in terms of where it comes from
